Artist Statement

As someone who grew up playing video games most of the time, I created fond memories of these inspiring stories making it obvious to choose art as my focus. I knew I wanted to become an artist since I too can develop concepts that can give a person new memories and ideas they too can explore. I want to be a creator.


The Present

As a huge advocate for video games I now see myself as an artist who can use my now skills and aspiration a way to express storytelling through new media. Adobe, game engines, and video; in a new contemporary expression in ways I want the audience to gauge the mediums


A Future

My ultimate goal is to have people see video art and video games as the future since I believe storytelling and immersion are powerful in ways physical art cannot achieve. I want to be the person who can be model to others that they too with hard work in these mediums can make a change in this digital age.
